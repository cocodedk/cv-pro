## Phase 1: Foundation – Audit and Compliance (2-4 Weeks)
First principles: You can't fly if your ship isn't airtight. Europe, especially Denmark, is a regulatory fortress – GDPR, data protection, employment laws. One breach and you're done.

### Key Actions:
- **GDPR Deep Dive**: Audit your data flows. Your Supabase setup is good, but ensure user data (CVs, personal info) is encrypted end-to-end. Implement right to erasure, data portability. Musk lesson: At Tesla, we obsessed over safety first – here, privacy is your safety net.
- **Danish Market Research**: Denmark has a strong welfare system, high digital adoption, and values work-life balance. Research local job platforms (like Jobindex.dk), salary expectations, and cultural nuances. AI-assisted surveys: Use Grok-like tools to analyze 1000+ Danish CVs for patterns.
- **Legal Partnerships**: Hire a Danish EU GDPR expert. Cost: €5-10k, but saves millions in fines. Musk tip: Don't DIY legal – I brought in experts for Starlink's FCC approvals.
- **Tech Audit**: Scan for vulnerabilities. Your FastAPI backend is solid, but add rate limiting and audit logs. Test with Danish users for feedback.

**Milestone**: Pass a mock GDPR audit. Budget: €10k-20k.

## Phase 2: Product Polish – AI and User Experience (4-8 Weeks)

### Key Actions:
- **AI Integration**: Leverage xAI's principles. Add predictive job matching (e.g., "Based on your skills, apply here"). Use your existing AI service to generate cover letters in Danish/English. - **Localization**: Danish UI/UX. Translate everything, add Danish keyboard shortcuts, integrate with Danish banks (MobilePay). Cultural fit: Danes value equality, so emphasize fair hiring tools.

- **Performance Boost**: Optimize for mobile (Danes are mobile-first). Reduce load times; your Docker setup is good, but add CDN for Europe.

**Milestone**: 90% Danish user satisfaction in beta tests. Budget: €50k-100k (dev costs).
